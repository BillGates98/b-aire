The cerebella were dissociated with trypsin, plated on poly-l-ornithine-coated plastic dishes (Becton Dickinson, Franklin Lakes, NJ, USA) at 1.25 million cells/mL, and maintained in low-serum, nutrient-supplemented Dulbecco’s Modified Eagle Medium/F-12 (Life Technologies; 5% CO2, 37 °C) for 12 days.
